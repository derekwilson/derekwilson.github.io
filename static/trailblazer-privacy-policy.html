Trailblazer Privacy Policy

Trailblazer is an application written by Andrew Trevarrow and Derek Wilson.

This policy describes the data that is collected by Andrew Trevarrow and Derek Wilson.

Last updated: 1-Aug-2024

Summary

Trailblazer does not collect collect or share any Personal or Sensitive information

What information is collected?

None. No personally identifiable information is collected by the app.

How is this information processed?

No information is collected.

How long is the information kept?

No information is collected.

How can I delete my information?

No information is collected. When you uninstall Trailblazer everything it installed (the app) is removed.

User accounts

Trailblazer does not have user accounts, no login information is required or requested and thus user accounts do not need to be deleted.

Advertising

Trailblazer does not show any adds.

In-app purchases

Trailblazer does not allow any in-app purchases.

How do I know that this is true?

Trailblazer is open source and this repository contains the source code for the application. You can check the code.

Changes to the Privacy Policy

The policy may be updated from time to time to comply with changing regulations.
